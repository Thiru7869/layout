Pattu Saree

About Pattu Saree

The Pattu Saree is a traditional handwoven silk saree, known for its elegance and rich colors. It is a symbol of grace and is often worn during weddings and festive occasions.

This saree features intricate designs and is made from pure silk, making it soft, luxurious, and comfortable to wear. The vibrant colors and exquisite craftsmanship make it a timeless piece for any wardrobe.
