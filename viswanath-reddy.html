Viswanath Reddy

Viswanath Reddy is a skilled Kalamkari artist renowned for his ability to blend traditional art techniques with a modern perspective. His works have captivated art lovers and collectors across the globe.

Biography

Born in Andhra Pradesh, Viswanath Reddy started his journey in Kalamkari early in life, learning from seasoned masters. His work showcases intricate motifs and stories drawn from Indian culture and mythology, while incorporating contemporary elements into his designs.

Kalamkari Artist

Viswanath Reddy has dedicated his career to preserving and evolving the art of Kalamkari. His works not only display technical proficiency but also tell powerful stories, often inspired by historical and mythological themes. He has mentored numerous young artists, ensuring the continuity of this ancient craft.
